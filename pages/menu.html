how to realize your order?

To place your order, first add all the dishes you want with the Add to order. When you have completed your choice, scroll to the bottom of the page where you will find a summary of your order and where you can delete a dish if you wish. If you are happy with your order, you just have to touch the Send order to kitchen and gone!
